Onboarding Guide
Pre-Call Checklist
1. Answer within 3 rings — speed to lead is everything
2. Confirm the address and what they need painted
3. Ask: "Is this interior, exterior, or both?"
4. Ask: "Any specific colors in mind?"
5. Log ballpark budget if they offer it
6. Book the on-site appointment — give them 2 times to choose
7. Confirm your phone number and send a text after: "Hi [Name], it's Thomas from Gould Painting. Looking forward to meeting you [day] at [time]! — (716) 221-6418"
On-Site Estimate
1. Walk every room before measuring — understand the full scope
2. Measure walls (L × H), ceiling, and all trim
3. Note number of doors, windows, and coat counts
4. Take photos of every room (for reference + scope disputes)
5. Check Numbers tab to review labor, GP, and profit margin
6. Target: 40%+ GP margin. Below 30% — reconsider or adjust scope
7. Present quote clearly — don't apologize for the price
Closing the Job
1. After sending quote, follow up in 24 hrs: "Hey [Name], just wanted to make sure you got the quote. Happy to answer any questions!"
2. Second follow up at 3 days if no response
3. If they say yes — collect 30% deposit immediately
4. Mark Won → Build Work Order → Confirm sub
5. Never start a job without a deposit in hand
Sub Management
1. Send feeler texts 1–2 weeks before start date
2. Labor rate: $45/hr flat — no exceptions
3. Confirm sub 48 hrs before start — text them the address and start time
4. Text client day before: "Our crew will be there tomorrow at [time] — any questions?"
5. Punch list must be completed before final payment released
6. Pay sub within 3 days of receiving final payment
